    Snapshot of the five runtime state bags at the end of the turn, produced by the kernel. Widened in 0.7.x from the pre-F23 Mars-shape (flat population/morale/…) to a structural match of the universal schema's WorldSnapshot: metrics for numeric gauges plus optional capacities, statuses, politics, and environment bags populated when the scenario declares them. buildRunArtifact maps this directly onto per-timepoint WorldSnapshot objects without flattening.

    Back-compat: Mars + lunar scenarios still put population, morale, foodMonthsReserve, infrastructureModules, scienceOutput, births, and deaths under metrics, so legacy consumers reading ta.stateSnapshotAfter.metrics.population still work.
